Halo Live Host Rules

Presented by Cute Stars Agency

Welcome to the spotlight. Play smart, stay safe, and shine bright.

General Conduct

  • Be respectful – No insults, bullying, or discrimination
  • No nudity or sexual content – Violations result in bans
  • No political or religious discussions – Stay entertainment-focused
  • No fake identity – Use your real name and photo

Streaming Behavior

  • Be consistent – Stream at least 3–4 times per week, 60+ mins daily
  • Stay engaging – Smile, talk, and connect with your viewers
  • Use music wisely – Only royalty-free or app-approved
  • No screenshots or screen recording allowed

What Not to Do on Camera

  • No smoking, drinking alcohol, or showing drugs
  • No lying down or being off-camera
  • No beds or bedroom backgrounds

Earnings & Gifts

  • Gifts are real income – Engage meaningfully
  • Don’t beg for gifts
  • Don’t accept money outside the app
  • Scamming = permanent ban

Account Safety

  • Always use your real name and photo
  • Never share your login with anyone
  • Multiple violations lead to permanent bans

Tips for Success

  • Look polished – Good lighting, hair, makeup
  • Speak confidently and elegantly
  • Follow a daily routine: stream, update photos, reply to fans
Treat your stream like a business. You are the brand.
